
Dict Ex2
--------

a. Create two devices with the same key-value pairs as specified in the first dictionary
   exercise (dict_ex1). These two devices should be assigned to variables named: chi_rtr1,
   and chi_rtr2 respectively.

b. Create a new blank dictionary named net_devices.

c. Assign the two devices created in step-a to the net_devices dictionary using the keys
   "chi_rtr1" and "chi_rtr2" respectively.

d. Use rich.print to print out your net_devices dictionary.
